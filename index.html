The Walking Dead

Fight the dead, fear the living

The Walking Dead is an American horror television series directed by Frank Delabont and starring Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, Lauren Cohan, Steven Yuan, and others. Based on the comic book series of the same name, the series premiered on AMC on October 31, 2010 and is the first authentic zombie drama in television history.

Rick is a deputy sheriff in a small town outside Atlanta. In the line of duty, Rick was shot and seriously wounded. He was rushed to a local hospital. When Rick wakes up, the world has been overrun by zombies and is in danger.
